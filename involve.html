Membership

Becoming a member is pretty easy! Just come to our events, and you'll be one - none of those applications or formal induction ceremonies or whatnot needed. To be honest, they're pretty annoying.

But how do I know about ACM events?

Just visit our homepage for upcoming news and events! Or, better yet, sign up to receive emails from us! We send regular emails about upcoming events and opportunities, plus optional internship and job opportunities. You'll never need to come to us - we'll come to you.
