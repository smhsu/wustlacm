About

Science Olympiad is an American elementary, middle, and high school team competition in which students compete in 'events' pertaining to various scientific disciplines. Here at Wash U, we are affiliated with Campus Y and we go to a middle school to help teach kids teamwork, science, and engineering. And, these kids might win something in the process!
Visit the official Science Olympiad national home page

All volunteers must complete a background check and brief training done through the YMCA.
