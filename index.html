Welcome to ACM!
(Washington University in St. Louis Chapter)

We are ACM, or Association for Computing Machinery, and we're Wash U's premier computer science club. We do various activities to teach aspiring students more about computers and the tech industry, as well as provide a gathering of all-around cool, smart people. Whether or not you're interested in computers, you're welcome with us!

GPU Computing Presentation

Time: Thursday November 13, 6:30 pm
Location: Gregg Technology Classroom

At next week's T3, Gabe Hope will be giving an introduction to GPU computing! GPU computing is an exciting technology that has exploded in use in the past few years. It uses the same graphics processors that power your favorite video games to achieve massive speedups in other applications. Fields such as scientific computing, machine learning, computational biology, finance and more all use GPU computing. Come and learn how you can make some of your own programs over 10x faster!

As with all T3s, there will be free food!
