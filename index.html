Welcome to ACM!
(Washington University in St. Louis Chapter)

We are ACM, or Association for Computing Machinery, and we're Wash U's premier computer science club. We do various activities to teach aspiring students more about computers and the tech industry, as well as provide a gathering of all-around cool, smart people. Whether or not you're interested in computers, you're welcome with us!

Humans vs Zombies

Time: November 21, 7:00 pm

Location: Urbauer 222

This Friday, for one evening, we pit the humans against the zombies. Who will win? Will the humans survive in the allotted time? Or be consumed by the horde? All are welcome to play, and there will be prizes for all!

We meet at 7 in Urbauer 222 to discuss the rules and to select who gets to be Patient Zero. If you can't arrive at 7, no worries - just go to Urbauer 222 and we'll put you on a team.

Don't forget: bring balled up socks (or similar soft, ball-like objects) to use as weapons against the horde!
